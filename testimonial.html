TESTIMONIALS

Aisha Aijaj (2018-2019)

With some of the best equipment, dedicated libraries, placement opportunities, workshops, and techno-cultural fests, the Computer Science department of Jamia Millia Islamia has been an excellent form of exposure for me. I have had the pleasure of studying under and working with eminent professors who have relentlessly assisted me in my research and project works. The campus is beautiful and the atmosphere is always inviting.

Najeeb Khan (2018-2019)

My undergrad at Department of Computer Engineering, Jamia Millia Islamia has been nothing short of an insightful experience. The department provided me with a platform where I learned something new every day and helped me foster an environment of technological collaboration. It symbolised a point of stress in my life which head-started me onto a trajectory of innovation and creation. I would like to thank my fellow peers, my professors and the staff at the department for providing me unparalleled support, infrastructure and a plethora of opportunities that have helped me to excel in my career into the field of Computer Science.

Maria (2018-2019)

It was an honor to be a student of Jamia Millia Islamia, and to graduate as a computer engineer with a job in my hand. I appreciate all the faculty and staff members at JMI who have taken the time and made the effort to each me, and to help me develop my professional career. JMI has the highest standards of lectures by supportive and experienced professors, a friendly environment and highly computed labs. My knowledge considerably expanded in M.Tech and it encouraged me to obtain my PhD in the impending future.
